# Gigalogy Inc. Profile

- Pilot focus areas include role-based voice workflows, business FAQ handling, and guided operational support.

## Company overview
- Company name: Gigalogy Inc.
- Founded: January 17, 2020
- Headquarters: Tokyo, Japan
- Company type: Artificial Intelligence and Technology Company
- Core philosophy: User experience first

Gigalogy focuses on practical, scalable, AI-powered products that connect advanced technology with real user outcomes.

## Mission and meaning
- Mission: transform complex and unknown AI patterns into solutions that are easy to understand and usable for everyone.
- Name origin:
  - Giga: large number of patterns
  - Analogy: explanation through comparison

## Product lines
### Personalizer
Omnichannel personalization for e-commerce. Focus areas: product discovery, engagement, revenue, and customer lifetime value.

### Maira
No-code generative AI platform for business-aware assistants, company-knowledge question answering, recommendations, and guided journeys.

### G-Core
Fully managed AIOps infrastructure for hosting, training, deployment, and ML workflow integration.

### SmartAds
AI-driven programmatic advertising with campaign prediction, dynamic budget updates, cross-platform measurement, and ROAS optimization.
